  • Wide temperature range
  • Consistent quality

Torchseal — APP Modified Membrane

ADWAA TORCHSEAL is a prefabricated waterproofing membrane with a multilayered design of nonwoven polyester reinforcement coated on both sides with APP modified bitumen, for superior pliability, tensile strength, elongation and lap joint strength. Ideal for roofs, reservoirs, basements, tunnels and car parks.

  • Total impermeability
  • High tensile strength
  • Impact and puncture resistance
  • Simple torch installation
  • Granule or smooth finishes
  • ASTM / UEAtc / DIN references
Typical technical highlightsSoftening point ~155°C · Nominal weight 3.4–5 kg/m² · Nominal thickness 3.4–5 mm · Roll size 1 x 10 m · Complies with ASTM D6222 Type I, UEAtc, CGSB 37-GP-56M, DIN 52133.

Liquid cold applied waterproofing coating formulated from ductile, high quality asphalt, selected solvents and special additives, designed to withstand normal cycles of expansion and contraction.   • Long lasting protection

High performance acrylic coating that enhances reflectivity and provides long-term protection for roofs, water based and environmentally friendly with excellent adhesion.

  • High reflectivity
  • Water based & eco friendly
  • Excellent adhesion
  • Easy application

Roofcoat — Elastomeric Roof Coating

Premium quality oxidized bitumen coating manufactured from ductile, pliable bitumen and plasticizing oil, blown to optimum and uniform softening point. Used in built-up roof construction on inclines not exceeding 25% (Type IV / 115-15) and 12.5% (Type III / 85-25) slope.

  • Crack bridging
  • UV & weather resistant
  • Waterproof
  • High elasticity
Typical technical highlightsSoftening point 80–120°C depending on type · Flashpoint COC 235–240°C · Complies with ASTM D312 & BS 3690 Part 2.

Sungard — Bituminous Aluminium Coating

Cold applied, highly reflective asphalt base aluminium coating formulated with ductile, pliable bitumen blended with highly polished aluminium flakes. Used to preserve, protect and beautify smooth asphalt roofs, modified bitumen roofs and granule finished roofs, and to protect metal structures from corrosion.

  • Reflects up to 70% sunrays
  • Reduces surface temperature
  • Anti-rust & corrosion coating
  • Water resistant
Typical technical highlightsComplies with ASTM D2824 Type I · Packed in 20-litre pails and 200-litre drums · Coverage ~0.16 kg/m² on metal, 0.27 kg/m² on concrete.
