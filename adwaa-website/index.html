Company Profile

Quality. Performance. Reliability.

ADWAA Petroleum Products Factory is a manufacturer of premium bituminous products serving road construction, waterproofing and infrastructure sectors. Located in Jeddah Industrial Area 2, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, ADWAA combines modern manufacturing technology, strict quality control and experienced technical expertise to deliver reliable products for demanding projects.

Our commitment to innovation, consistency and customer satisfaction enables us to supply contractors, distributors and infrastructure developers across regional and international markets.
